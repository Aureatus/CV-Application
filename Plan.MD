# CV Application

## Sections
1. General Information
  - Name
  - Email
  - Phone Number
2. Educational experience
  - School name
  - Title of study
  - Date of study
3. Practical experience
  - Company name
  - Position title
  - Main tasks of positions
  - Dates you've worked for companies
